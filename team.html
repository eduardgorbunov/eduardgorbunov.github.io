Application checklist

What to include

When writing to me, please keep the message specific and include the following information.

  • Subject lineUse a clear title such as "PhD application - Applicant Name" or "MSc inquiry - Applicant Name".
  • MotivationExplain the research motivation and how it connects to my research directions.
  • BackgroundSummarize relevant experience in optimization, machine learning, mathematics, or adjacent areas.
  • DocumentsAttach a CV and, for student applicants, an academic transcript.
  • Research alignmentBriefly mention relevant papers from the publication list and how they connect to the proposed research direction.

Due to the high volume of applications, not all inquiries can receive an individual response.
