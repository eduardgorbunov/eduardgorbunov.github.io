How to join/visit my group

Postdoctoral Researchers. If you are interested in a postdoctoral position, please submit your application via the following link: https://apply.interfolio.com/170202.

Interns and Visiting Students. If you wish to join as an intern or a visiting student, please apply using this link: https://apply.interfolio.com/170203.

PhD Applicants. If you are interested in pursuing a PhD under my supervision, please send me an email briefly explaining your motivation and attach your CV and academic transcript. Additionally, you must apply through the official
MBZUAI website.

Master's Students. If you are interested in joining MBZUAI as a Master’s student, please apply directly via the
MBZUAI website.

Please note: Due to the high volume of applications, I may not be able to respond to all inquiries individually.
